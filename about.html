ABOUT HYDRAULICS

The term "Hydraulics" in the context of clothing actually refers to a few different things. Most commonly, it's the name of a high-end fashion retailer in South Africa, but the term can also relate to technical workwear or specific product features.

1. Hydraulics Store: The most well-known reference to "Hydraulics" in fashion is the Hydraulics Store, a popular South African fashion retailer that offers a range of clothing, footwear, and accessories for men and women. The store is known for its trendy and contemporary styles, often featuring streetwear-inspired designs.


Hydraulics (Luxury Fashion Store): This is the most likely meaning. "Hydraulics" is a South African concept store chain that stocks a curated selection of prominent international luxury brands. They are known for high-quality, exclusive products and a focus on streetwear and avant-garde fashion.


2. Hydraulics in Workwear: In a more technical context, "hydraulics" can refer to clothing designed for workers in industries that involve hydraulic systems (such as construction or manufacturing).
